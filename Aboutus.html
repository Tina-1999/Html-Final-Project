Our Story

Ting's Hanfu Clothing was founded in 2023 by ShuTing, a lover of traditional Chinese clothing. After struggling to find high-quality, affordable hanfu dresses for herself, she decided to start her own business. Today, Ting's Hanfu Clothing is a leading provider of hanfu dresses for women.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make traditional Chinese clothing accessible to everyone. We believe that everyone should be able to experience the beauty and elegance of hanfu dresses, regardless of their background or budget.

Our Team

At Ting's Hanfu Clothing, we have a dedicated team of designers, tailors, and customer service representatives who work hard to ensure that every customer is satisfied. We are passionate about hanfu clothing and are committed to providing the best possible experience for our customers.
